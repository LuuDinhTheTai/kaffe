Shipment Information

Delivery Times

We process all orders within 24 hours of receiving them to guarantee maximum freshness.

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Next Day Air: 1 business day (orders must be placed by 12 PM PST)

Rates

We offer competitive shipping rates to make sure you get your coffee affordably.

  • Standard: $4.99
  • Expedited: $9.99
  • Next Day Air: $24.99
  • Free Standard Shipping on all orders over $50!

Return Policy

If you're not fully satisfied with your order, please contact our support team within 14 days of delivery. For safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on roasted coffee, but we will happily replace your order or issue a refund if something isn't right.
